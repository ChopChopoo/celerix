Frequently asked questions

Common questions about Celerix shoes and ordering.

How should I size the Celerix Apex?

The Apex fits true to size for most runners. The forefoot is moderately tapered to suit a racing fit. Wide-foot runners may prefer a half size up.

How many miles can I expect from the Apex?

We design the Apex for 250 to 300 miles of racing and key workouts. The PEBA-blend midsole holds its energy return well within that range.

Is the Apex suitable for a first marathon?

Yes, provided you have done long runs in a carbon-plated shoe before. We recommend at least two 16-mile runs in the Apex before race day.

What is the difference between the Apex and the Apex SR?

The SR has a stiffer plate and a 4 mm lower stack. It is built for distances up to a half marathon where the lower mass helps more than the larger stack.

Where are Celerix shoes manufactured?

Our shoes are assembled in Vietnam from materials sourced in Japan and South Korea. Final QA is done at our San Diego facility.

What is the return policy?

We offer a 30-day return on unworn shoes. Race-day shoes can be returned within 14 days if you have logged fewer than 20 miles in them.
