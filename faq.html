Frequently asked questions

Common questions about Celerix shoes and ordering.

What is a helically-wound carbon rail?

Rather than a flat carbon plate, the SpiralCore is a full-length carbon rail wound in a gentle spiral, so it flexes and rotates with the stride instead of locking the foot flat.

Is the Spiral a race shoe?

No. The Spiral is a plated daily trainer. The helically-wound rail is built for a forgiving everyday ride, not race-day snap.

Can I buy the Spiral now?

Not yet, but it goes on sale to the public soon. For now only early testers have a pair.

How long does the Spiral last?

We are still collecting long-term mileage from testers; early data looks steady through the first few hundred miles.
