Celerix

Energy return, measured.

Celerix builds carbon-plated racers for distance runners who want a predictable, repeatable response over 90 minutes or more. The VectorReturn plate sits in a PEBA-blend midsole that is stiffer in the forefoot and softer in the heel, a geometry chosen after two years of in-house biomechanics testing. The brand was founded in 2023 by a former materials engineer and a road marathoner who met at a running camp in Mammoth Lakes. The line is intentionally narrow: two race-day shoes and one tempo trainer, all built around the same plate geometry.
