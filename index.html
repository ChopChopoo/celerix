Celerix

A spiral carbon rail, not a flat plate.

Celerix is a small brand started in 2025 around a single design question: can a carbon plate stop feeling like a stiff lever? Their answer is the SpiralCore, a full-length helically-wound carbon rail laid in a gentle spiral rather than flat. Where a flat plate locks the foot into one motion, the spiral rail flexes and rotates with the stride, so the shoe stays forgiving on easy miles instead of snapping back on every step. It is built as a plated daily trainer, not a race shoe. Celerix is not out yet, but the first batch goes on sale to the public soon; for now it is only with early testers. The range is one daily trainer offered in three weights, all on the same helically-wound rail.
